How to use this?
To use Kleara, a topical acne treatment, cleanse your face thoroughly, then apply a small amount of the gel directly to individual pimples or affected areas, once or twice daily as directed by your doctor or the product instructions; be sure to avoid contact with healthy skin and eyes.

1.Clean skin: Always start with a clean face before applying Kleara.
2.Spot treatment: Only apply a tiny amount of gel directly to the pimples, not large areas of skin.
3.Do not over-apply: Too much Kleara can irritate healthy skin.
4.Follow instructions: Consult your doctor or read the product label for the recommended frequency of use.
